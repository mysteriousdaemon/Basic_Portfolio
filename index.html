About Me

In his endless search for truth man is condemned to knowledge. Everything else is whim. Whenever we show pity, we ravage ourselves. We must ask ourselves: is that which is indispensable to life also harmful to it? When man is happy, the meaning of life and other eternal themes rarely interest him.

So, are we concerned with happiness or the eternal?
